About TexElite Construction

TexElite Construction has been serving the North Texas area since 2006. We specialize in full-service commercial and residential construction. Whether it is a custom kitchen renovation or a large scale commercial construction project, we complete every job with commitment and integrity.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and our customers has been key to our success. We take tremendous pride in every project and have built our company through word of mouth, and customer testimonials and referral.
